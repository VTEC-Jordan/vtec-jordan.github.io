Consulting & Venture Building

How we engage

VTEC's consulting and venture-building service area is structured around three engagement tracks. Each is designed for a different relationship type, commitment level, and business stage. Pick the one that fits where you are, or reach out and we'll help you decide.

Track 01 Pay As You Go

Hourly Consulting

For organizations that need expert guidance on a specific challenge or a short-term initiative, without a long-term commitment.

Typical work includes

  • Product strategy reviews
  • Technology assessments
  • Digital transformation planning
  • Startup mentoring
  • Product roadmap development
  • Team coaching
Engagement model Billed per hour. Start with a defined problem, engage as needed. No minimum commitment. You pay for the time you use.
Get started
Track 02 Monthly Retainer

Strategic Retainer

For companies that need ongoing strategic support without the cost or overhead of hiring a full-time executive or product team.

Typical work includes

  • Product management advisory
  • CTO or product leadership support
  • Product roadmap planning
  • Technology strategy
  • Process improvement
  • Team mentoring
  • Stakeholder management support
Engagement model Fixed monthly fee for a predefined number of consulting hours. Consistent senior access without a full-time hire.
Get started
Track 03 Partnership

Venture Building Partnership

For startups and founders looking for a long-term strategic technology partner, not just a vendor. VTEC acts as an active co-builder, not a hired contractor.

What we bring

  • Business and product strategy
  • Market validation
  • Product discovery
  • UX and UI design
  • MVP development
  • SaaS platform development
  • Technical leadership
  • Product management
  • Growth planning
Engagement model No upfront development fees. VTEC provides services on a deferred-compensation basis and receives 20% of the technology budget allocated from successful funding rounds, subject to mutually agreed contractual terms. Your cash stays with you until you raise.
